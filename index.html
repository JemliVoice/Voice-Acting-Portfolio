Meet Jemli

Voice Actress, Writer

Elizabeth Stokely, also known as Jemli, is a voice actress with a passion for good stories and bringing them to life. 

Story-telling has been a part of human history since before we could even write. Elizabeth understands and appreciates how important it is to share with one another the human experience through story-telling. By working with her, know that your creative voice will be heard.
