My name is Elizabeth Stokely, and I'm a voice actress/voice over artist, based in the US!

I am bilingual, speaking English and Russian natively, and I can also do a variety of accents and characters; from a little cutie to a serious survivalist, or even fantasy creatures.

My vocal range is E4 to E6, soprano. My acting experience comes from theater; mostly from local Russian community plays. 

I have a home studio, so my best work is recorded remotely. I would also be available for remote live direction.
