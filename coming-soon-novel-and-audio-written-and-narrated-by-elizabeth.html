COMING SOON: NOVEL AND AUDIO, WRITTEN AND NARRATED BY ELIZABETH

Keep an eye out for my upcoming novel, Say Cheese Inc. I will have the audio narration here, on my website!

Something strange is inside Cheese Inc., the popular and ever-growing social media company's headquarters...

or is it all just in Trinity's head?

Have you ever seen something out of the corner of your eye, something you're sure you saw but knew that it couldn't have been there? This is what it is like every day for Trinity, a troubled young secretary who has landed her first job at the largest and most advanced social media company in the world as of 2039: Cheese Inc. Everything about the corporation's headquarters is impressive; like the the Feed-integrated personal elevators--but it's not until Trinity finds one man's name on a document that her entire understanding of who she really works for changes. Trinity must find a way to rise above her limitations and see past her condition in order to find the truth in this technologically-impressive world.
